FAQ

Questions that usually come up before we start.

Do we need to have a CRM already?

No. The system can start simple and grow based on your team size, workflow, and lead volume.

Is automation suitable for a small business?

Yes. Small teams often benefit the most because their time and attention are usually limited.

Will every message be handled by a bot?

Not at all. Good automation helps define when the system should respond and when a real person should step in.

Can we begin with an audit first?

Absolutely. A funnel and workflow audit is often the safest first step before building a larger system.
